## This project (HaleHound Fire)

**HaleHound Fire** is an independent open-source **Fire OS / Android companion app** for Amazon Fire tablets (validated on Fire 7 12th gen `KFQUWI`). Authors: PascalKeeper / Joseph Peransi.

It provides:

1. A VALHALLA-style legal/authorization gate  
2. **Passive** Blue Team Wi‑Fi and BLE survey on stock Fire OS  
3. Local Network Guardian (no laptop required)  
4. Best-effort discovery, telemetry probe, and loot offload for a **separately flashed** ESP32 CYD running official HaleHound firmware  

It does **not**:

- Ship or rehost official HaleHound firmware binaries  
- Phone home or phone the official project  
- Claim to be the official product, or to replace ESP32 CYD hardware for multi-radio modules
