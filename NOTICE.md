## This project (HaleHound Fire)

**HaleHound Fire** is an independent open-source **Fire OS / Android companion app** for Amazon Fire tablets (validated on Fire 7 12th gen `KFQUWI`).

It provides:

1. A VALHALLA-style legal/authorization gate  
2. **Passive** Blue Team Wi‑Fi and BLE survey on stock Fire OS  
3. Best-effort discovery / browser open for a **separately flashed** ESP32 CYD running official HaleHound firmware  

It does **not**:

- Ship or rehost official HaleHound firmware binaries  
- Claim endorsement by the HaleHound creators  
- Replace ESP32 CYD hardware for multi-radio offensive modules
